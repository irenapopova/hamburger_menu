Camping & Hiking

The Black Forest

Hiking is an adventurous experience. Germany’s iconic Black Forest has been the inspiration for the famous Brothers Grimm fairytales and other German myths and legends. Located in the southwest in the state of Baden-Württemberg, it’s one of the most popular hiking and trekking locations for outdoor pilgrims. The area is packed with mountainous terrain, miles of breathtaking forest dotted in between with charming little villages. There are three main long-distance hiking routes; the Westweg trail in the west, the Mittelweg trail in the central forest, and the Ostweg trail in the east.
